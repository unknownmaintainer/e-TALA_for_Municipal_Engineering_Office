About the Platform

e-TALA (Electronic Tracking, Archiving, and Location Architecture) is the official digital management system designed specifically for the Municipal Engineering Office of Carigara, Leyte. It streamlines building permit applications, tracks municipal & barangay infrastructure projects, geocodes records onto interactive GIS maps, and enforces compliance against unpermitted illegal construction.
